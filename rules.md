A few simple laws keep the hunt fair and the quality supreme.

## The code

Solve clues in order — each one points to the next. Leave every station as you found it; a good hunter disturbs nothing. Work in teams of any size, but only one team touches a clue at a time.

## Reveals

Every ticket carries its answer under a green wax seal. Lifting it costs you nothing but pride — use it only when you're truly stumped.

## Winning

The first team to reach the final marker and recite the SQuaSH motto claims the prize. Ties are settled by riddle-off.
